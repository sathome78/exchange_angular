{{'PROJECT INTRODUCTION' | translate}}

The Diamond Open Market is EOS-based platform for everybody to buy and sell natural and lab-grown diamonds. DOM will present a marketplace for business and individuals working as a reliable tool for B2B or P2P relations. The platform will gather individual diamond holders, buyers, cutters, jewelers, as well as everybody involved in the gemstone business to agree on the prices and build a transparent and fair diamond market, offering a variety of payment methods, including cryptocurrency transfers.

{{'KEY FEATURES FOR USERS' | translate}}

  • 1. Fair prices.

    The diamonds you may buy in jewelry stores are overpriced, because their production is controlled by the monopolists. Diamond Open Market will form an alternative market by working with small-scale producers and independent diamond resellers.

  • 2. A marketplace, operating worldwide.

    No need to buy from the local companies, find the best offers in any country of the world.

  • 3. Natural or lab-grown diamonds are available in one place.

    The DOM platform will allow users to choose between the natural or lab-grown diamonds from the best manufacturers and individually agree on the prices. It will also present the whole range of related services, including the shipping, evaluation and certifying of the stone.

  • 4. Variety of payment methods.

    The counterparties might agree on different paying methods, including banking, cash or cryptocurrency transactions.

  • 5. No fees or commissions.

    DOM will be built on EOS blockchain ecosystem and operate without any fees for both counterparties.

  • 6. Professional search and selection system.

    If you have no knowledge about diamonds or real asset trading experience, you can easily become a pro with our advanced and intuitive search and selection system.

{{'KEY FEATURES FOR TRADERS' | translate}}

  • 1. Sell to anybody in the world.

    You can expand the geography of your trades by using Diamond Open Market.

  • 2. Build your business instantly with DOM value-added services

    The DOM platform’s value-added services can support any business and individual traders’ needs. These services include relevant search, storage, insurance and shipping services as well as ads and promotions on the platform. The services can be paid with DOM tokens.

  • 3. Personal manager support and arbitration system.

    DOM will help to solve any issue and provide a 24/7 personal manager support.

  • 4. Secured smart contracts, allowing to implement any business logic.

    The safety issue can easily be solved by using smart contracts. This way the seller will not receive payments until the service or product is provided or delivered. Every transaction might be tracked with the help of blockchain. Complex deals can also be done using the individual smart contract that might be adjusted to every business needs.

  • 5. Mobile version and a messenger.

    DOM will meet the highest requirements of convenience being presented in desktop and mobile versions and translated to 16 languages. Another feature will be the messenger inside the platform, which can be used among counterparties to make contacts and agree on deals individually.
